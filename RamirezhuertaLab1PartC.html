Recipe For Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 8oz Chicken Breast
  • 1 cup Rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • Oil of choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Add oil to pot and pan
  2. chop chicken breast into cubes
  3. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, chili powder, and garlic powder in desired proportions
  4. Add the rice and water to the pot
  5. Set pot burner to high heat until boil
  6. Once water is boiling, top the pot with a lid and set burner to low heat
  7. Let rice cook for 20 minutes
  8. Add the seasoned chicken to pan
  9. Set pan burner to medium heat for about bout 5 minutes while constantly mixing
  10. After 5 minutes, set pan burner to low heat and let cook for about 20-25 minutes
  11. Once both rice and chicken are cooked, serve yourself a plate and enjoy!
